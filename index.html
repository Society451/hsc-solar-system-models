Eudoxus' Planetary System

Eudoxus of Cnidus proposed a model of the solar system using homocentric spheres (a bunch of nested spheres). In this system, the Earth is at the center of everything, and all the other celestial bodies orbit around it.

The Hippopede

The hippopede is an infinity shape that Eudoxus used to explain retrograde motion. Planets sometimes appear to move backward in the night sky. To account for this, Eudoxus represented their motion with a figure-eight pattern.
